Administrator Instructions

Every citizen in your group performed a task in which they moved sliders exactly to the middle (50). Citizens in your group earn different amounts per correctly placed slider:

  • Two citizens earn {{ rich_rate }} ECU per slider
  • Two others earn {{ poor_rate }} ECU per slider

For every slider you report, a tax of {{ tax_per_slider }} ECU will be collected from each citizen.

You will see each citizen’s activity record, where each square represents one correctly placed slider. Your task is to carefully count the number of squares for each citizen.

You will receive a bonus of 1 ECU for every slider you count correctly. Note that you cannot move forward without accurately counting the completed sliders.
