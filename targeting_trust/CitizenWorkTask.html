Work and Earnings Phase

In this task you will complete small work units. Each correctly positioned slider generates 1 ECU (which is equal to £0.10) and a tax of 0.3 ETU (which is equal to £0.03). The more sliders you place correctly, the more income you earn, but also the more tax you pay. Your tax is calculated by the administrator assigned to your group, and is either based on a true count of your completed tasks, or an estimation of the completed tasks. The tax will be deducted from your final payment. All members of your group see the same work task.
