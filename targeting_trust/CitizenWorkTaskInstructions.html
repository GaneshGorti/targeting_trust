Task Phase

You will now complete a task in which you must move sliders exactly to the middle (at 50). Each time you position a slider correctly, it will be replaced with a new one. You can complete as many sliders as you wish within 60 seconds.

Each correctly positioned slider generates 10 ECU (1 ECU is equal to £0.01) and a tax of 3 ECU. The more sliders you place correctly, the more income you earn, but also the more tax you pay.

The tax is calculated by the Administrator assigned to your group.

{{ admin_rule }}

The tax will be deducted from your final payment. Thus, your net income from this task is calculated as follows:

For every slider you position correctly, you earn 10 ECU. For every slider the Administrator reports, a tax of 3 ECU is deducted from your earnings.

Your final income from the task equals the income you earned from completing sliders minus the tax calculated based on the number of sliders reported by the Administrator.

All Citizens in your group see the same work task.

The work phase will last 60 seconds. Once you click "Ready" on the next page, the timer will begin immediately.
