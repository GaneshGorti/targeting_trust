Task Phase

You will now complete a slider task. Your goal is to move each slider exactly to the middle (50). Each correctly placed slider is replaced with a new one. You have 60 seconds to complete as many as you can.

Your piece rate is {{ piece_rate }} ECU per correctly placed slider (1 ECU = £0.01). Note that in your group, 2 citizens earn {{ rich_rate }} ECU per slider and 2 citizens earn {{ poor_rate }} ECU per slider — you are one of the {{ piece_rate }} ECU citizens.

After the task, the Administrator will count your completed sliders and report the number. A tax of {{ tax_per_slider }} ECU is then deducted from your earnings for every slider reported. The Administrator receives a bonus of 1 ECU per slider counted correctly, and the system does not allow misreporting.

Your net income from this task is calculated as follows:

For every slider you position correctly, you earn {{ piece_rate }} ECU. For every slider the Administrator reports, a tax of 3 ECU is deducted from your earnings.

Your final income from the task equals the income you earned from completing sliders minus the tax calculated based on the number of sliders counted and reported accurately by the Administrator.

All Citizens in your group see the same work task.

The work phase will last 60 seconds. Once you click "Ready" on the next page, the timer will begin immediately.
