Example: How your bonus and citizens' taxes are calculated

Citizens in your group earn different amounts per correctly placed slider:

  • Two citizens earn {{ rich_rate }} ECU per slider
  • Two others earn {{ poor_rate }} ECU per slider

Regardless of the rate they earn per slider, each citizen pays a tax of 3 ECU per slider reported.

Example — If a citizen correctly placed 10 sliders and you correctly report 10, the tax would be:
10 × 3 ECU = 30 ECU, regardless of what they earn per correctly placed slider.

You receive a bonus of 1 ECU for every slider you count correctly. If you correctly report 10 sliders for each of the 4 citizens, your bonus would be:
4 (citizens per group) × 10 (correctly placed sliders per citizen) × 1 ECU = 40 ECU.

If you have understood the instructions and the example, click Next.
